PJPT Overview#

TCM Security’s Practical Junior Penetration Tester is a training program designed to offer hands-on penetration testing material and labs. The course covers essential tools and techniques that are exercised through labs and taught through lectures. PJPT focuses on developing the ability to identify and exploit vulnerabilities.
